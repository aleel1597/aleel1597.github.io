  • 2006 Our second family startup, Zyno Medical, a drug infusion medical device venture is founded. I am now in third grade, coming to the office regularly to help around the office.

  • 2014 A spin off of Zyno, Infutronix is founded and launches its new medical device, the Nimbus ambulatory infusion pump.
