My professional life at a glance ↓

  • 1997 Born in Boston MA
  • 2003 My father bootstraps CareEverywhere, a SaaS B2B medical device software platform. Funded with a second morgage on our home, this was when I was introduced to startups.
  • 2006 Our second family startup, Zyno Medical, a drug infusion medical device venture is founded. I am now in third grade, coming to the office regularly to help around the office.
  • 2012 I spend my first summer working full time at Zyno on the engineering team. My involvement within my family's startups becomes real and helping in whatever free time with anything needed becomes the norm.
  • 2014 A spin off of Zyno, Infutronix is founded and launches its new medical device, the Nimbus ambulatory infusion pump.
  • 2015 I get my first non family startup internship at Verizon as a UI/UX Intern, High school graduation, and I start college at Univeristy of Illinois at Urbana Champaign
  • 2017 I start working my first real external job at SIEMENS Healthineers as a Software Engineer Co-Op building software for CT scanners.
  • 2018 My next job leads me to Blue Cross Blue Shield where I become a mix of a Business Analyst, Prototype Software Engineer, and a Product Manager.
  • 2019 I graduate from Univeristy of Illinois at Urbana Champaign with a Bachelors in Computer Engineering. I start my new job at IBM as a Technical Solutions Specialist.
  • Beyond More adventure awaits!
